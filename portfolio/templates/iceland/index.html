Reasons to pick us.

Read below about what it means to choose Iceland as your trip organiser.

Anywhere, Anytime.

Pick any location in the world, it could be the Sahara, Yosemite or the Alaskan mountains. Go wild. It can also be anytime, the cold snowy winter or the blazing hot summer, the cool but warm spring and the brown chilly Autumn.

Bring only the essentials

Don't worry if you dont have all the specialist gear, we will tell you what to pack and what you don't have we will provide. If you want you may come only in a suit. You don't have to worry about forgetting anything

It's all about you

You decide where you want to go and what you want to do. We cater for all fitness levels. We will customise the trip to your needs and wants.

Push yourself

Push yourself a little further. Go a little further. Climb a little higher and stay a little longer. See your limits. Experience things you haven't before. Live the adventure while you can. Have a story to tell and make memories that will last.

Take the road less travelled

We don't go by road, there is no footpath, there is no track, path or trail. We make our own. And that's amazing, go to places where other's haven't been. See things no one has seen. Discover yourself and the world around you.

Wonders of nature await

See the things you see in nature magazines, be one with nature. Open your eyes and take it all in. Gaze at the night sky and see more than just stars, see the whole universe. It's all within your reach.

What are you still waiting for?

You should find out more by chatting with us,
and hopefully let us help you plan an amazing trip.
