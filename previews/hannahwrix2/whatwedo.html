Our Garda Vetting and screening process

Every member of our community goes through a rigorous screening process which includes Garda Vetting. Here's how it all works.

Our Garda Vetting acts as umbrella vetting. This means that childcare professionals who are part of our Community can work in different settings and locations while using this Garda Vetting.

When you book a Childcare professional through Last Minute Minders you will be given access to all the documentation that you require in order to be compliant. This will include:

  • Garda Vetting
  • Copy of all qualifications
  • Two verified Childcare related references
  • Photo ID
  • CV
  • Any other relevant certification

Benefits of offering your Employees support with their childcare

Staff Turnover reduced

  • Among employees with children, 26 percent report they have considered leaving their employers due to child care issues.
  • After the first year of employment, employee turnover is most likely to occur among parents of young children.
  • Of employees with children in an employer-sponsored child care solution, 93 percent reported that child care was an important factor when considering a job change. Just under 20 percent had actually turned down a job offer rather than lose their child care.

Reducing Absenteeism

  • Parents in employment have child care problems, on average, six times a year, with 98% having to take time off as a result.
  • Child care issues account for 24% of absences in the workplace.
  • Absence now costs companies between €500 and €600 per employee per year
