What we do to support you...

We employ a community of amazing minders nationwide who provide childcare support to parents, creches, organisations, hotels and their guests.

Providing excellent childcare is our priority and to ensure our high standards are met, all of our minders are rigorously screened before they are available to work.

We are the only agency in Ireland who conducts extensive background checks on all of our minders including Garda Vetting, qualification checks, reference checks and interviews offering you the highest quality minders, eliminating any stress!

Supporting Creches

With over 20 years’ experience in the Childcare industry we know that staffing is often an obstacle in providing quality childcare..

Here’s how we can help you!

We can provide your crèche with:

  • Relief Cover
  • Maternity Leave Cover
  • Holiday Cover
  • Summer Work for your Minders: if your Creche closes for the summer and your staff are seeking work during these months, we can help!

When you book a Childcare professional through Last Minute Minders you will be given access to all the documentation that you require in order to be compliant including:

  • Completed Garda Vetting
  • Qualifications
  • Two verified Childcare related references
  • Photo ID
  • CV

Once you are registered with us, you can begin to request cover immediately.

Weddings & Events

LMM Care can provide a full Pop Up Creche service to help with 5 to 50 children at your wedding or celebration. Let us look after your little VIPs so that your event goes off without a hitch and everyone has happy memories of the day!

We can also provide babysitters if you are attending a family wedding or function out of town. LMM Care can provide a minder for your children in your hotel room while you attend a wedding or event.
