A little bit about us

Last Minute Minders is Ireland's safest way to source your childcare for your family or your business. We give peace of mind by conducting extensive background checks on all the childcare professionals in our community including Garda Vetting, reference checks and interviews.

Last Minute Minders came to life in 2007 and was established by Hannah Wrixon who recognised a need for staffing support in the childcare industry.
