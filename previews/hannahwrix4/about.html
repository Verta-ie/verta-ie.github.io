Our Mission

Last Minute Minders came to life in 2007 and was established by Hannah Wrixon who recognised a need for staffing support in the childcare industry.

Hannah ran a successful crèche in Limerick for a number of years and knows the difficulties that face childcare providers around staffing, in particular maintaining ratios in the event of staff absences. As a parent of three Hannah also recognised the struggles that all working parents face around childcare. Last Minute Minders was initially born to address these needs.

We have adapted and grown the business in line with our customer’s needs over the years and now offer a suite of services to support them.

Last Minute Minders is a community of childcare professionals who are Garda vetted, qualified, reference checked and interviewed. Our rigorous pre-screening means that only the very best minders are accepted into our community.

We with our Minders have the knowledge and experience to offer solutions that work for you.

What we do…

We are an introductory agency with a community of dedicated minders nationwide who provide childcare support.

We introduce users of our service to these amazing minders for relief and emergency support.

Each minder has an online profile where they can set their availability, preferred work locations and manage their work.

Our community of Minders have gone through our rigorous screening process to become a member. This means that they are now available to support creches, hotels and parents.

All of our minders are self employed contractors who work independently.
